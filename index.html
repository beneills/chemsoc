Who are we?

ChemSoc is a student-run society for Southampton University Chemistry students.

We run socials all year, whether that's organising nights out, arranging paintballing trips or the hugely popular Summer Ball, we truly have something for everyone! Alongside our regular socials, we also provide support to help further your academic progress through activities and workshops. Get involved and seize this unique opportunity by joining ChemSoc today!

We also have a variety of sports teams that participate in University tournaments, so check out our Sports page!



If you havent already, you can join our Facebook page as an easy way to get all updates!



We understand how difficult and confusing it can be in the first few weeks of being at a new university, and so we have a parenting scheme in place where Year 1 students are partnered with Year 2 students, to offer you support. If you need any other help, feel free to get in contact with any committee member or email chemsoc@soton.ac.uk.
